At Spicy Taco, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and share your personal data when you use our products.

Reimag(in)e App

Reimag(in)e is a mobile application that allows you to upload photos and generate AI-powered images using OpenAI's technology. We want to be completely transparent about how your data is handled:

  • No Image Storage: Photos you upload are not stored in our database. They are only used temporarily to generate AI images and are immediately discarded.
  • Generated Images: AI-generated images are not stored in our database. All generated images are only saved locally on your device.
  • No Personal Information: We do not collect or store any personal information from Reimag(in)e users in our database.
  • Third-Party Processing: While we use OpenAI's API to generate images, no personal information is passed to OpenAI. Only the uploaded photo and generation prompts are sent for processing.
  • Device-Only Storage: All images (both your original photos and generated images) remain exclusively on your device. You have full control over your data.

Reimag(in)e is designed with privacy in mind - your photos stay on your device, and we don't track or store your image data.
